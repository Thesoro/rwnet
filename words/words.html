Rob writes words. He got a degree in Drama from the University of Washington in 2009. He writes comedy and also does improv with Jet City Improv and Unexpected Productions. Improv shows he's been in include:
  • House of Ink (UP, 2014)
  • Final Fantasy: The Improvised Adventures (UP, 2012, 2014)
  • It's Your Wonderful Life (JCI, 2012, 2013)
  • Final Transmission (JCI, 2011)
He also writes sketch comedy and has done so for the youtube channel Satire and the live comedy group The Pilot Episode. He has written for Wizards of the Coast and the web series Job Hunters.
